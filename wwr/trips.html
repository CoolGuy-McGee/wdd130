Trips Available

Guided

The guided trip is for those new to whitewater rafting and would like to know how to do it. An instructor will accompany your party on the raft so you can learn the ropes, as well as have a good time.

Intermediate

The intermediate trip gets you on rougher waters due to your thrill seeking personality. An instructor will accompany your party on the raft so you can learn from the best on the quicker moving waters.

Adventurer

The Adventurer trip is also on rougher waters like the Intermediate trip. This time you don't have an instructor to accompany your party because you know what you are doing. Since we don't provide you with an instructor, we do provide you with savings; this is the cheapest package we have available.
